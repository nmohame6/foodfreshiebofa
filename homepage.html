Did You Know?

An estimated 23 percent of college students (3.8 million) experienced food insecurity in 2020, according to GAO's analysis of student data from the Department of Education's National Postsecondary Student Aid Study (NPSAS).

Our goal is to do our part to alleviate the burden that food insecurity to students attending UNC Charlotte.
